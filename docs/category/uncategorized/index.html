Introducing our closing keynote speaker: Jenny Wong

Jenny Wong

Keynote Speaker
@miss_jwo

Jenny is a Community Engineer at Human Made. Her love for development goes beyond the screen & she is an advocate for both the PHP and WordPress communities.

As a speaker, community event organiser and PHP Women evangelist, you can usually find her loudly cheering people on to share knowledge & contribute back to their communities.

PHPConf.Asia adopts a Code of Conduct

We are pleased to publish our official Code of Conduct for all attendees, exhibitors and all our conference staff. Our Code of Conduct is adapted from PyCon Code of Conduct and the GeekFeminism.org Code of Conduct.

In it, we detail our definition of harassment, methods of reporting, appropriate response from our staff and participants and also the consequences (if any).
